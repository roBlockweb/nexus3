How Nexus Works

1
Capture

Click the Nexus icon or use shortcut (Ctrl+Shift+S) to save content from any web page.

2
Process

Nexus uses on-device AI to analyze and categorize your content, extract key entities, and find connections.

3
Connect

Your knowledge is automatically connected in a network you can explore visually.

4
Discover

Find insights, patterns, and relationships in your knowledge network.
